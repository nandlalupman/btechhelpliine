College Onboarding Instructions

Reach engineering aspirants direct on BtechHelpline

To ensure accurate filtering and premium display on our student dashboard, please complete the onboarding checklist. The wizard contains 8 sections:

  • Step 1: Basic Profile - Standard name, year, governance, autonomy, and NIRF rank fields.
  • Step 2: Branch Seat Matrix - Dynamic addition of branches, total seats, and quota splits.
  • Step 3: Entrance Cutoffs - Accepted exams (JEE/State) and previous year opening/closing ranks.
  • Step 4: Financial Matrix - Honest fee breakdown (Tuition, Caution deposit, Hostel AC/Non-AC, and Dev fees).
  • Step 5: Placement Analytics - Placed %, median/average packages, and mass recruiter details.
  • Step 6: Lifestyle & Rigor - Curfew policy, dress code strictness, attendance rules, and club counts.
  • Step 7: Media & Infrastructure - Upload widescreen profile banner and facility photos (labs, classrooms, library).
  • Step 8: Verification Attestation - Attest submitted details and complete authorization.
